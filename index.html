StyleScan.

About

StyleScan is an upcoming mobile app that scans real word product, traditional advertisements and logo's, and not only finds the product, but also produces similar alternatives.



With StyleScan you're able to easily scan and buy your favourite fashion, be it in commercials, your friends style or search for differently priced alternatives at the store.
